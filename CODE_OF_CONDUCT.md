# Code of Conduct

## Our Philosophy

This is an open and collaborative space where everyone is welcome to learn, share, and grow.

There is no pressure to perform, no expectation to be perfect, and no judgment for where you are in your journey.

Whether you're just starting or already experienced, the goal is simple:
**build, learn, and help each other.**

---

## 🤝 What We Encourage

- Sharing your projects freely, no matter your level
- Asking questions without fear of judgment
- Giving constructive and respectful feedback
- Helping others learn and improve
- Taking initiative and contributing ideas
- Being supportive and patient with fellow members

---

## 🚫 What We Don't Accept

- Harassment, discrimination, or offensive behavior of any kind
- Toxic criticism or disrespectful communication
- Gatekeeping or making others feel unwelcome
- Unsolicited pressure or unrealistic expectations
- Any behavior that discourages others from participating

---

## 💬 Communication Guidelines

- Be kind and respectful
- Assume good intentions
- Give feedback in a helpful and constructive way
- Remember: everyone is learning no mather your age or experience life's a constant learning process

---

## 🧠 Contribution Mindset

You are free to:
- Create and share your own projects
- Explore and learn from others' code
- Suggest improvements or ideas

You are **not required** to:
- Contribute to everything
- Be active all the time
- Know everything

Participation is voluntary — contribute in the way that works best for you.

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

If someone behaves in a way that goes against this code of conduct, maintainers may take appropriate action, including:
- Warning the member
- Removing content
- Restricting or removing access to the organization

---

## ❤️ Final Note

This community exists to support growth, not perfection.

Be respectful. Be curious. Be helpful.

And most importantly — enjoy the process, happy coding to us all.
